Can AI to be your junior accounting partner?

AI has the potential to be a game-changer, making tax knowledge more accessible and easing the workload for tax professionals. But, can an AI solution effectively handle the real-life tax situations faced by individuals and businesses alike, from unique deductions to determining dependents? Let's explore this together.

What is this solution?

Like ChatGPT, Tax AI is built on a version of Open.AI’s GPT AI. Unlike ChatGPT, Tax AI supports accounting work. How? Starting with general internet data from the GPT model, Tax AI was further trained with Australian Tax information (including case law and reference materials). When engaged by a firm, Tax AI is then trained by the firm’s own work products and templates (much like a new employee’s onboarding when joining the firm!).

Tax AI assists with accounting, audit, bookkeeping, and regulatory compliance and can help generate insights, recommendations, and predictions based on data. As a result, accountants can deliver faster and more cost-effective solutions for client issues. It's like having unlimited entry level accountants!

When will I get an answer?

You'll receive the AI accountant's answer within ~10 seconds. It will appear right below the area where you typed your query.
